BeeTraffic App


BeeTraffic is an application that facilitates communication between Bee Farmers and the Swiss Bee Traffic Agency. Enter a previous source, the destination site, and the number of bee colonies. Automatically, the inspectors will be notified and you will receive confirmation whether you can move bees. One can select the site on the map and also save them for later use. The app is currently available on Google Play Store and App Store and was developed with Identitas Switzerland.
Development Platform: Xamarin Forms, Xamarin Studio, Visual Studio, C#, .NET

ERNI Translate app


ERNI Translate App is a mobile app which supports OCR and language translation service thus enabling users to convert digital image into a translated text. Users can select an image, convert it to an editable text, and translate to a supported language. Technology used: MS Cognitive Service OCR API, MS Translate API, and Image processing (custom renderer for iOS and Android image cropping).

Blood Glucose Monitoring App


This app is used to facilitate the daily monitoring of blood glucose levels. I recreated an existing Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) library used to connect to the glucometer. Aside from recreating the library, I also improved BLE pairing between app and device.


Development Platform: Objective-C, Xcode

AC Hub App


“ACHub” is an enterprise iPad application used by the AC Hub staff since August 2015 at their various stations throughout Algonquin College. I individually designed and developed this app in the summer of 2015, which was a huge learning opportunity.
